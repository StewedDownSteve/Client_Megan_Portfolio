National Song Contest Voting Mobile App

Summary

At Sidebench, I spearheaded the creation of a National Song Contest Mobile App at Sidebench. This innovative app enabled over a million users to participate in real-time voting during the contest, employing gamification to incentivize user engagement and strengthen their votes. The user-friendly profiles and real-time voting capabilities transformed the contest experience, fostering community and excitement while ensuring a sustainable and seamless voting process.

My Role

Client Relations: Strengthened client relationships by building rapport, communicating effectively, proactively managing expectations, and skillfully eliciting and understanding client requirements in a way that fosters trust and allowed for consistently delivered results that aligned with clients' needs and expectations.

Market Research & Partner Sourcing: Conducted thorough market research, assessing third-party companies to enhance localization and robust voting capabilities. This informed data-driven decisions by providing insights into industry dynamics, competition, and user experiences.

Results

Increased User Engagement: Through gamification, user engagement was significantly enhanced, encouraging active participation and voting.

Client Satisfaction: Effective client relations led to strengthened client relationships, fostering trust and satisfaction.

Efficient Voting Process: Real-time voting capabilities and user-friendly profiles ensured a smooth and seamless voting experience.

Data-Driven Decision-Making: Market research and partner sourcing provided critical insights for informed decisions, enhancing localization and voting capabilities, and contributing to the project's success.
