Shoe Exchange Mobile App

Summary

At Sidebench, I played a key role in developing a Shoe Exchange Mobile App aimed at connecting sneaker enthusiasts and facilitating transactions within local communities. Inspired by platforms like OfferUp, the app enabled users to create profiles, including ratings, reviews, and shoe listings. It streamlined the exchange and sale process, allowing for communities to sustainably source sneakers.

My Role

Market Research: Conducted market research incorporating data analysis, competitor research, and usability testing, yielding valuable insights into industry dynamics, competitive landscape, and user experiences and facilitating data-driven decision-making.

Product Strategy: Crafted wireframes, diagrams, user flows, and story mapping in Miro, while capturing the essence of user interactions and design concepts for product features.

Results

Competitive Edge: Thorough competitive research and analysis have identified opportunities, providing the app with a competitive edge and a unique value proposition for users.

User-Centric Design: The app boasts a user-centric design achieved through wireframes, diagrams, user flows, and story mapping, ensuring an interface that aligns with user interactions and enhances user-friendliness.

Efficient Exchange Process: Team contributions have led to an efficient and user-friendly exchange and transaction process, enhancing the user experience.

Sustainable Community Building: The app's localization features foster sustainable sneaker sourcing within communities, promoting an engaged and vibrant user community.
